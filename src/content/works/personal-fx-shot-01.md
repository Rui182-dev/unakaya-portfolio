## Overview

This shot was designed to show how multiple simulation layers can work together within a single beat. The goal was not only to create destruction and energy effects, but to keep the sequence readable as each element entered the frame.

## Breakdown

I built the main FX work in Houdini, focusing on rock breakup, energy motion, lava interaction, smoke shaping, and the timing relationship between the layers. The shot was treated as a compact reel piece, so the pacing of each element was adjusted to read quickly without feeling rushed.

## Technical Focus

The main technical challenge was keeping the shot cohesive while several effects overlapped. I focused on balancing primary motion with secondary detail so the sequence felt layered rather than noisy.
