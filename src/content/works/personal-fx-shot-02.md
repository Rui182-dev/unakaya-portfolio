## Overview

This shot explores the impact of a large mechanical form entering the ocean. The main goal was to create a clear and convincing water silhouette at the moment of contact, then support it with layered secondary spray and surface breakup.

## Breakdown

I approached the sequence as a study in timing and scale. The primary splash needed to feel heavy and directional, while the surrounding whitewater and mist had to support the motion without overwhelming the frame.

## Technical Focus

The most important part of the setup was separating the larger readable forms from the smaller detail layers. That made it easier to control the overall shape of the impact and keep the shot visually clean in motion.
